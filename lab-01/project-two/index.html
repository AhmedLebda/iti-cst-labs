Exercise 1: "About Me" Page

Create a page named aboutme.html that describes you. On your page, include some or all of the following information:

  • Your name
  • A describtion of yourself in two sentences or less
  • a list of classes you are taking right now at temple.
  • your 3 favorite movies, books or tv shows, in order. Make atleast one link to an interesting site about that tv show, such as it's IMDB
  • Two images, one that represents you when you're happy and the other to represent you when you are sad'
    (These can be any images you like. consider searching for images on Google Image search )
  • Something interesting about one or more of your neighbors (people sitting at computers next to you)

(You may be tempted to spend all 50 of your minutes on this part of the lab.)
